# Our Data Sources as a Strategic National Asset

Aotearoa New Zealand has world-leading public data, so how do we realise the
value of these data assets?

**Thursday 9 March 2023, Te Papa Tongarewa Museum of New Zealand, Wellington**

Te Rourou Tātaritanga and the Social Wellbeing Agency present an opportunity to listen and engage with internationally recognised authorities on linked administrative data, including the Integrated Data Infrastructure (IDI).

In this Symposium you will hear from leading experts in data infrastructure, artificial intelligence, advanced analytics, and privacy, presenting and discussing how innovative approaches could realise the value of our national data assets.

Confirmed speakers include:

* Professor Julia Lane
  New York University, Cofounder Coleridge Initiative
* Nancy Potok
  Director Data Foundation, former Chief Statistician of the USA
* Bill Simpson-Young
  CEO Gradient Institute
* Professor Chris Dibben
  Director Scottish Centre for Administrative Data Research, Co-Director International Population Data Linkage Network

Please join us for kōrero, good coffee, and kai. This is a free event, but
places will be limited, so please register your interest on our website:
https://terourou.org/symposium2023.
