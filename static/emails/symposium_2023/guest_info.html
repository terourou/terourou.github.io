Venue Information
The symposium will be held in the “Oceania” room on Level 3 of Te Papa, and reception will open at 8.30 am. On arrival, please check in at the registration desk and collect your lanyard.
There will be complimentary espresso coffee and tea available throughout the day with catering during the breaks.

Programme Information
The programme will start at 9.00 am with a Mihi Whakatau, introduction and scene-setting, and an opening by the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Social Development and Employment, Carmel Sepuloni.
There will be two breakout sessions in which all attendees can engage in a conversation and share their thoughts, experiences, and ideas.
In the afternoon, we will host a panel discussion before closing the event at 5.00 pm. A more detailed timeline with session topics can be found on our website.
